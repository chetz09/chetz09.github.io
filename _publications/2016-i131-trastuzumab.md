### Hypothesis
¹³¹I-Trastuzumab can be produced with high radiochemical purity and immunoreactivity to selectively target HER2-positive breast cancer for radioimmunotherapy.

### Approach
- Iodination of Trastuzumab using Iodogen method.
- Radiochemical QC: purity, stability, immunoreactivity against HER2-positive cells.
- Biodistribution in tumor-bearing mice.

### Key findings / conclusion
¹³¹I-Trastuzumab showed selective uptake in HER2-positive xenografts with acceptable stability and immunoreactivity, establishing a foundation for preclinical HER2-targeted radioimmunotherapy studies.
