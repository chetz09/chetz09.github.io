### Scope (review)
Synthesizes current MRI-CEST approaches for probing the metabolic hallmarks of cancer — glucose uptake, lactate/acidosis — using endogenous and exogenous CEST contrast.

### Topics covered
- Physical basis of CEST and Z-spectrum analysis.
- Endogenous (APT, amide, glucoNOE) and exogenous (iopamidol, iobitridol, glucose analogues) agents.
- Protocols for acidoCEST and GlucoCEST.
- Clinical translation challenges: B0/B1 correction, specificity, scan time.

### Take-home
MRI-CEST provides a radiation-free, quantitative means to interrogate tumor metabolism and acidosis, with a growing toolkit of agents and analysis methods bringing it closer to routine clinical use.
