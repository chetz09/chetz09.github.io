### Hypothesis
Conjugation of Rituximab to the kinetically stable CHX-A''-DTPA chelator and radiolabeling with ¹⁷⁷Lu will produce a radioimmunoconjugate with high immunoreactivity and targeted tumor uptake suitable for Non-Hodgkin's Lymphoma (NHL) radioimmunotherapy.

### Approach
- Immunoconjugate synthesis: CHX-A''-DTPA conjugation to Rituximab.
- ¹⁷⁷Lu radiolabeling, radiochemical purity, and *in vitro* stability.
- Immunoreactivity (Lindmo), *in vivo* biodistribution, and preclinical dosimetry.

### Key findings / conclusion
The ¹⁷⁷Lu-CHX-A''-DTPA-Rituximab conjugate was produced in high radiochemical yield, retained immunoreactivity, and showed favorable tumor uptake profiles — supporting its development as a CD20-targeted radioimmunotherapeutic for NHL.
